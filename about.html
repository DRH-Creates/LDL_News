Lubbock Drifting League

The Lubbock Drifting League (LDL) is a premier motorsports organization dedicated to the art of drifting in Lubbock, Texas. Founded in 2026, LDL has quickly become a hub for car enthusiasts and professional drifters alike, offering thrilling events, competitions, and a vibrant community for all things drifting. With a focus on promoting safe and exciting drifting experiences, LDL hosts regular practice sessions, local competitions, and special events that showcase the skills and passion of drifters from across the region. Whether you're a seasoned pro or new to the world of drifting, the Lubbock Drifting League welcomes you to join the excitement and be part of our growing family of motorsport enthusiasts.
